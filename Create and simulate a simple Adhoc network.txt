AIM :- Create and simulate a simple Adhoc network. 

-------------------------------------------------------

Step 1:- From the bottom toolbar, click on ‘Network Devices’ then click ‘Wireless Devices’
	and select ‘AP-PT’ Access Point.

Step 2:- From the bottom toolbar, click on ‘End Devices’ and select ‘PC’ and then click on
	the screen (for 5 PC’s do this step five times).

Step 3:- Now, we will make the PC wireless, for that Click on PC, dialog box will appear.
	In dialog box, in Physical section, first turn off the power button then remove the ethernet port
	from PC and assign the ‘PT-HOST-NM-1W’ port, then turn on the power button.

Step 4:- Similarly, Do this with Every PC.

We have successfully connected with every device.
